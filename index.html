MISSION STATEMENT

The cardinaln aim and objectives of the fellowship as pointed out in article 2 section 1 of the Y.F.C.F igalogba constitution drafted in 2014 states, to train and develop individual members in the doctrin of cristianity with a view of attaining an standard required of a true follower of christ by making themselves usefull not only to the church and community in which they live,but also the nation, so that they may allways strive daily to "maximimizing the youthful days and living for gods purpose". To actualize this,the following stategies are adopted:

  1. WORSHIP:to encourage corporate/individual worship and prayers
  2. SERVICE:to lead and encourage the youth to enlist themselves in the service of Godas true,dutiful and honest members of the church.
  3. THOUGHT:To enable the youth to learn individually and collectively,the mearning of the christian faith,its relevance to theirgeneration and to practice same in their day to day life.
  4. to propagate the gospel of christ through sacred music,personal and mass evengelism,drama and charity
  5. to provide a forum where the youth can meet and share their christian experiences(Heb 10:25)

    HISTORY OF YFCF IGALOGBA

    History of youth for christ fellowship,united evangelical church, igalogba,idah kogi state.
    The fellowship as its name implies is a gathering of young men and young ladies who are baptiezed into the united evangvelical communion or are undergoing the trainning,with the cardinal aim of enhancing spiritual relationship with God and their young canterpats,maximixing youthful days and fufilling destinies. The fellowship was inagurated in 1995 after the planting of united evangelical church igalogba the same year,her first president being Elder joseph odekina who was succeded by Elder friday Ekele,followed by Dcn onechojon okoliko,mr.udale agbenyo,mr.Timothy sule,mr.jonathan opaluwa and currently,Bro. monday opaluwa. the fellowship through God's mercies and benevolent love has raised people of integrity, who are vibrant tool in the hands of God,and are doing wonderfully well in their respective field of endevours,profession and callings,and have become societal transformer wherever they are found.
